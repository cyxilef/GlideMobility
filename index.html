Our Mission

Glide Mobility is dedicated to empowering independence and mobility for disabled individuals, with a special focus on serving rural communities across the South and Midwest.

Who We Serve

Our primary mission is to provide innovative mobility solutions to disabled individuals, particularly those in group homes and hospitals. We understand the unique challenges faced by people with disabilities, especially in rural areas where access to advanced medical equipment can be limited.

Our Commitment

We recognize that cost and insurance coverage are significant concerns for our community. That's why we've developed comprehensive solutions to make our products accessible:

  • Insurance Coordination: We work directly with healthcare providers to assess patient needs through prescriptions and medical reports, helping coordinate insurance coverage.
  • Financial Assistance: Income-based discounts and payment plans are available for those who qualify.
  • Accessibility First: Disabled individuals receive priority pricing and support, ensuring our products reach those who need them most.
  • Rural Focus: We're committed to serving rural communities where mobility solutions are often harder to access.

Our Approach

We work closely with healthcare providers to ensure our wheelchairs reach those in need. Through our diagnostic lab, we assess eligibility for discounts and coordinate payment through insurance when possible. For those who don't qualify for insurance coverage, we offer flexible payment options based on credit qualifications.

Inclusive Design

While our primary focus is on disabled individuals, we welcome anyone who can benefit from our technology. Non-disabled customers can also enjoy our products for leisure purposes, though they will not receive the same discount levels as disabled customers.

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to continuous product development. During the first few months after purchase, we actively follow up with customers to address any issues and gather feedback to improve our products and services.

Insurance & Payment Options

Insurance Coverage

We work directly with healthcare providers to help you get coverage through insurance:

  1. Consult Your Healthcare Provider: Discuss your mobility needs and the Glide Mobility wheelchair
  2. Medical Assessment: Your provider will assess your needs through a prescription and medical report
  3. Submit to Our Diagnostic Lab: Your provider sends the prescription and medical report to our lab
  4. Eligibility Review: Our lab reviews your case for insurance eligibility and potential discounts
  5. Insurance Coordination: If approved, we coordinate payment through your insurance provider

This process helps ensure those in dire need receive the support they require.

Credit-Based Monthly Payments

If insurance coverage isn't available, we offer credit-based monthly payment plans:

  • Credit check required to determine eligibility
  • Flexible monthly payment options based on your credit score
  • If you don't qualify for credit-based payments, insurance or other payment options are still available

Income-Based Financial Assistance

We offer separate income-based discount programs for those in financial need:

  • Assessment based on household income
  • Consideration of household size
  • Student status taken into account
  • Separate from insurance process

Contact us to learn more about income-based assistance programs.
